Delicious Lasagne Recipe

Below is the recipe and instructions for creating this fantastic tasting Lasagne that will be an all-round crowd pleaser!

This recipe serves 6 as a standard

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 diced carrot
  • 1 diced celery stalk
  • 2 sliced garlic cloves
  • 250g beef mince
  • 250g pork mince
  • 1 bsp tomato puree
  • 400g chopped tin tomatoes
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 200ml red wine
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 8-12 lasagne sheets (depending on dish size
  • 50g parmesan grated
  • 150g shredded mozzarella

For the white sauce

  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 550ml sem-skimmed milk

Now lets cook it up!

  1. In a large frying pan heat the olive oil on a low heat and then fry in the onions, carrot, celery and garlic for 5 mins.
  2. Add the minace and fry until golden
  3. Turn up the heat and pour in the wine, pure, stock and chopped tomatoes
  4. Add in the worcesteshire sauce and simmer that bad boy on a medium heat for 15 mins.
  5. Meanwhile, make the white sauce. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over a low heat and add the flour. Whisk until combined and cook on low for 1-2 mins.
  6. Remove from the heat and gradually whisk in the milk until you have a loose sauce and then season and whisk until thickened on low heat
  7. Preheat the oven to gas 6, 200°C, fan 180°C. Layer up the lasagne in a baking dish, starting with a third each of the ragu, then the pasta, then the white sauce. Repeat twice. Top with the Parmesan and mozzarella then bake in the oven for 40-45 mins, until piping hot and crisp and bubbling on top. Serve immediately.
