A beautiful chicken curry

This is a fantastic family curry that is easy and quick to make but also tastes fantastic and will surely be a staple in your weekly cooking repertoire

Ths recipe serves 4 as standard

Ingredients

  • 4 bonless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp tumeric
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • half tbsp ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • a splash ofolive oil
  • 1 chopped red chilli
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 100ml water
  • Rice as necessary
  • Season as needed with salt/pepper/corriander

How to prepare

  1. Cut the chicken into diced chunks
  2. Season the chicken with turmeric, chili powder, cumin, garam masala, ginger, and white wine vinegar, and mix wel
  3. Heat oil in a pot over a medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until brown.
  4. Add the chili, chopped tomatoes, water, salt, and pepper.
  5. Mix well and bring to boil
  6. Simmer for an hour or until sauce thickened
